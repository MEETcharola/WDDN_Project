-----------------------------------------------------------------Project Title : Result Portal----------------------------------------------------------------------

				         --DESCRIPTION--
=>	This is a result portal from where students can see their results online. Results are uploded by result_uploder.
Admin creates student profile. Uploader edits profile of student.

Technologies : HTML,CSS,BootStrap,ASP.net C#, MVC 5, Entity Framework(6.x)

Controllers : 1) adminController
	  2) studentController
	  3) result_uploaderController
                   

Views : 1) Student
	-studentlogin
	-details
            2) result_uploader
	-edit
	-index
	-uploader_login
           3) Admin
	-createuploder
	-createstudent
	-deleteuploader
	-deletestudent
	-index
	-loginadmin
	-viewstudent
	-viewuploader

Model : DataBased first approached is used.
